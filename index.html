60:40 Advantage

Africa is home for 60% of arable land but accounts for 10% of agricultural output.

6% Utilization

Only 6% of Africa's arable land is currently being utilized.

70% Dependance

70% of Africa's population relies on agriculture for their livelihood.

85% Food Imports

More than 80% of the food we eat is imported and this number has tripled recently.

Our solution

A sustainable future for the African economy!

We are building the African small scale farming ecosystem, 1 farmer at a time. De-risking them for investment.

Who we are

An all rounded fintech solution specifically for the marginalized and excluded small-scale farmers.

Our why

We exist for the purpose of driving these small-scale farmers into commercialization, while encouraging them to commit to climate-smart farming practices.
